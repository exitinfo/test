Computer Systems Lab @JBNU

 

The Computer Systems Lab at Chonbuk National University was founded in 2010, currently being led by Prof. Heeseung Jo.

Our research areas are centered around the computer systems architecture and systems software, which is, in nature, an evolving multi-disciplinary research area encompassing computer architecture, operating system, computer networks, etc. The main research goal is to bridge the gap between applications and hardware effectively by improving the design and implementation of computer systems in terms of performance, cost, reliability, and energy consumption.
